My favorite game


Badminton is a racquet sport played using racquets to hit a shuttlecock across a net. Although it may be played with larger teams, the most common forms of the game are "singles" and "doubles. It is the most accessible racquet sport universally, badminton has come a long way. It might even be the new favourite sport in the country. But did you know that badminton finds its origin in India?

Bonus tip: To end a rally and win a point, it is essential to learn how to smash the oncoming shuttlecock. There are techniques to return a smash as well. Some of the other most common shots include drop shot, lift shot, net shot and toss, all of which work best when played with the right technique and strategy.
